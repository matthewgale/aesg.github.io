About Us

About AESG

Maintaining your Essential Safety Measures (ESM) ensures that you are safe in case of fire. At AESG we will help you solve the “What,” the “Why” and the “How” of Essential Safety Measures.

We have been providing building consultancy services to the managed property industry since 1996. While our clients have a wide variety of building compliance issues, our experience has given them confidence that AESG audits are comprehensive and professional.

Insite Architects

AESG is associated with Insite Architects. This association enables AESG to access the expertise of the architectural firm, particularly in relation to defects reports that we produce, in our recommendations for remedial work or building notice rectification required by municipal councils.

Visit Insite Architects at www.insitearchitects.com.au

Kim Rockman, Managing Director

Kim has been an Architect and building practitioner since 1988 and for the past 21 years has been involved in the inspection and compliance auditing of buildings. Between 1990 and 1996 Kim consulted to Archicentre undertaking building inspections and the training Architects in inspection techniques. He founded AESG in 1996.

Kim has been instrumental in educating the managed property industry in the understanding of building compliance through the AESG training program. He brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to this field
